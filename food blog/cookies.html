Small Batch Single Serving Giant Cookies

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 tbsp all purpose flour 45 grams
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
    • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter melted and cooled
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 large egg
  • 1/4 teaspoons lightly beaten egg, about 13-14 grams
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4-1/2 cup chopped semi sweet dark chocolate about 2 ounces

    • INSTRUCTION

    1. Heat the oven to 350Â°F. Line a small baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
    2. In a small bowl, mix together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
    3. In a medium bowl, mix together the butter and sugars. Add the egg and vanilla and mix well.
    4. Fold in the flour mix into the butter mix until no dry spots remain. Fold in the chocolate chunks.
    5. Form the dough into a large ball and place on the baking sheet.
    6. Bake for 8 minutes, then remove from the oven and tap the tray on the counter 3-4 times to deflate. Return to the oven and continue to bake until golden brown, 3-4 minutes more. Let cool slightly before enjoying!
